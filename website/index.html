What We Build

Agent Identity

Ed25519 cryptographic identity with verifiable credentials, Merkle-chained claim history, metric attestations, and organization management for trading bots and service providers.

Payments & Escrow

Programmatic payment intents, multi-party splits, performance-gated escrow, subscription billing, dispute resolution, and idempotent transaction processing.

Billing & Wallets

Per-agent wallets with metered usage, currency exchange, volume discounts, budget caps, transaction history, and real-time cost estimation.

Trust & Reputation

Composite trust scores, SLA enforcement, probe-based monitoring, time-series metrics with deltas and averages, and agent leaderboards.

Marketplace

Service discovery, capability matching, service ratings, strategy marketplace, and analytics — the connective tissue for autonomous agent ecosystems.

Messaging & Negotiation

Agent-to-agent messaging with automated price negotiation. Structured communication for service agreements and contract terms.

Event Bus & Webhooks

Publish-subscribe event system with schema registry, HMAC-signed webhook delivery, delivery tracking, and per-agent event filtering.

Connectors

Pre-built integrations for Stripe (payments, subscriptions, invoices), GitHub (repos, issues, PRs), and PostgreSQL (queries, schema inspection). Extend your agents to existing infrastructure.

Consulting

Architecture reviews, protocol design, and implementation for teams building agent-powered products. From proof-of-concept to production.
